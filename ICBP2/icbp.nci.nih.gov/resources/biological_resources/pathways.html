Pathways

The following is a list of databases/websites containing information about pathways that are important in cancer processes:

  • Biocarta
    Pathways and the tools to study them; from Biocarta.
  • CGAP_Biocarta
    Cancer Gene Anatomy Project website: pathway information provided by Biocarta
  • KEGG
    Kyoto Encyclopedia of Genes and Genomes (KEGG) website: Wiring diagrams of molecular interactions, reactions, and relations. KEGG Pathway is a collection of manually drawn pathway maps representing our knowledge on the molecular interaction and reaction networks for:

    1. Metabolism, Carbohydrate, Energy, Lipid, Nucleotide, Amino acid, other amino acid, Glycan, PK/NRP, Cofactor/vitamin, Secondary metabolite, Xenobiotics

    2. Genetic Information Processing
    3. Environmental Information Processing
    4. Cellular Processes
    5. Human Diseases
    6. Drug Development
  • CGAP_KEGG
    Kyota Encyclopedia of Genes and Genomes (KEGG): pathways on the Cancer Gene Anatomy Project website.
