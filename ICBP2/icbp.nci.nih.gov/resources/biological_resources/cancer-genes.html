Cancer Genes

  • CGAP
    The Cancer Genome Anatomy Project
  • LocusLink
    Entrez Gene is a searchable database of genes, from RefSeq genomes, and defined by sequence and/or located in the NCBI Map Viewer
  • Waldman Cancer Gene List by Locus
    Cancer Genes and loci, listed alphabetically
  • The Tumor Gene Family Databases
    The Tumor Gene Family of Databases contains information about genes which are targets for cancer-casing mutations; proto-oncogenes and tumor supressor genes
  • CancerGene
    CancerGene is a computer program for estimating the likelihood that a family carries a mutation in one of the cancer predisposition genes
  • Atlas of Genetics and Cytogenetics in Oncology and Haematology
    A peer reviewed on-line journal and database in free access on internet devoted to genes, cytogenetics, and clinical entities in cancer, and cancer-prone diseases
  • Myc Cancer Gene Database
    Supported by the National Library of Medicine
  • NCBI dbSNP
    Database of "Single Nucleotide Polymorphisms", supported by the National Center for Biotechnology Information, NIH
